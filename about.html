Our Story

Founded in 2020, our company started with a simple mission: to make mosquito exterminating technology accessible and useful for everyone. Tatiana and Nathaniel met during a mosquito apocalypse and realized their shared vision of creating products to solve mosquito problems.

We believe in transparency, creativity, and continuous improvement. Our commitment is to bring the best experiences to our customers while fostering a supportive work culture.
