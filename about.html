Our Story

Founded in 2020, our company started with a simple mission: to make mosquito exterminating technology accessible and useful for everyone. Tatiana and Nathaniel met during a mosquito apocalypse and realized their shared vision of creating products to solve the mosquito problems. Over the years, they built a dedicated team, launched innovative solutions, and grew into a community-focused brand.

We believe in transparency, creativity, and continuous improvement. Our commitment is to bring the best experiences to our customers while fostering a supportive work culture.
