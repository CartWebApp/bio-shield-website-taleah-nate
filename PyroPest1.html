How to Use

Step into the flames (safely) and use your PyroPest T5 V1 like a pro:

  1. Charge the PyroPest T5 V1 for 2-3 hours (don’t skip this step unless you enjoy working with half a flamethrower).
  2. Flip the safety switch to “ON” (we're serious about safety here). No accidental fireballs!
  3. Hold the nozzle 4-8 feet away from your target. Aim it like a bug-hunting marksman.
  4. Press the trigger and let the flames do their magic. Say goodbye to bugs!
  5. Remember: You're holding fire—use it responsibly. This is a tool of extermination, not a party favor!

Important Warnings:

  • Do NOT aim at any living creature, including bugs and any unsuspecting neighbors.
  • Do NOT use near combustible materials, such as gasoline. (unless you want your lawn to be the next fire festival).
  • Keep out of reach of children and pets (We learned our lesson). They don't need to be learning about fire safety with your flamethrower. wait....
